About Us

At TechniCleanSun, we are a robot development company working for advanced solutions, modern, mainly innovating panel cleaning to improve solar energy efficiency.

Solar energy is a critical resource for meeting today's energy demands in a fast and flexible manner. But the cleanliness of solar panels is directly related to their energy efficiency. To solve this problem, we represent a big step in the field of research and technology "TechniCleanSun Robot", which works autonomously, has many sensors on it, and with which you can learn all the necessary information. Our brand is used for the production, sale and rental of these robots.

Your Security

Cleaning solar panels is a time-consuming and dangerous process. The fact that the solar panels are located in high places and the cleaning process requires special equipment and professional skills makes this task even more difficult. Therefore, to solve this dangerous process in a safe and effective way, our brand "TechniCleanSun Robot" provides innovative and easy-to-use solutions.

The TechniCleanSun Robot is small and light in size and can be autonomously controlled remotely. And thanks to its user-friendly interface, this robot, which can be controlled remotely and operates autonomously, ensures the safety of people and minimizes risks where it is operated. Safety and performance are within your reach with the TechniCleanSun Robot!

About Robots

Our main goal is to revolutionize the field of panel cleaning to increase the efficiency of solar energy.

To solve this problem, we introduce a product called "TechniCleanSun Robot". This robot works completely autonomously and has many sensors on it. You can learn all the necessary information through these sensors. Our brand is engaged in the production, sale and rental of these robots.

"TechniCleanSun Robot" has a light weight, which makes the robot easy to move and install. Also, this robot stands out for being practical and affordable. Through its powerful sensors, cleaning time, charging, speed and other functions can be controlled remotely, which gives great convenience to users. Our main difference is that the wheels rotate 360 ​​degrees and provide all-round cleaning, which ensures perfect cleaning of every point of the panel surface.
